About Me

Throughout my life, I have been around many different animals- from dogs and cats, birds, reptiles, hamsters, gerbils, guinea pigs, and to ferrets, just naming a few! Currently, I have a big-headed pitbull named Bruce Banner, and a bearded dragon named Swayze.

Growing up, I had started my own business at the age of 12 walking dogs. Many asked me why I wanted to do that at such an early age; my response was never because of the money, or because I was made to do so- it was because I had a passion for being around animals. I didn't feel capable of much being so young and inexperienced, but I made fliers and put them up in my neighborhood community billboard.

I didn't think anyone would give me a chance, being so young and with handling someone else's pet, but I was wrong! A kind couple hired me! They had told me they had a miniature schnauzer, had worked a lot, and needed someone to walk their dog. I quickly took that opportunity, and it changed my entire perspective on animals.

I had some knowledge on her breed and her personality because my family had a schnauzer as well, but it became much more than that. She was my first eye opening experience to realizing that every animal has very distinct and different personalities. Avery, the miniature schnauzer, was my client for 2 years before I had to move away. After Avery, my new neighbors hired me to take care of their chihuahua, Rottweiler, and Yorkshire terrier. This was a new challenge to me having 3 dogs at a time, but I was successful. Everyday, I let the dogs out, played with them for an hour, and fed them.

While having these 3 clients, I then got hired by someone to walk a Bully. I then had 4 different animals that I took care of. I had my daily routine for how I handled each client and their pets needs. I took care of all 4 of those dogs for 2 years. Unfortunately, I had to put a hold into my dog care business entering high school, and dedicating my time to marching band. Having this business experience taught me a lot of what I know on my animal care knowledge and helped me develop into who I am today.

Outside of my business, I have also taken care of many different animals for friends, family, and friends of family. Each experience teaches me something new, and I wish to grow and expand my knowledge everyday. I believe that just like people, animals have their own personalities and feelings too. I want to help make a difference in animal care. I want to continue my business that I had started as a kid, but with a much bigger perspective and with much more experience and knowledge. My business may have stopped due to school and extra curricular activities, but my passion for animals continues to grow everyday.

I want to go into how much I will love your pets, but I will just let my work speak for itself!

-Coral B., Pawfect Petcare
